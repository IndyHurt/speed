How fast can you drive in your area?

Welcome to our Targeted Editing series where today you can fill up an ice chest, or make OpenStreetMap data the best! (both take about the same amount of time)

  1. How can maxspeed be used? Maxspeed is an OpenStreetMap tag that is used to record speed limits for roads.

    1. Help calculate more accurate time of arrival estimates. Maxspeed is synonymous with speed limit. How long will it really take to get from point A to point B? Time is money! Maxspeed is just one attribute that contributes to more accurate time of arrival estimates for routing.

    2. Contribute to apps that display speed limits to help drivers avoid speeding. Do you travel often? Getting a speeding ticket in a city you are not familiar with is the worst. There are lots of applications that display the posted speed limit as you are driving. Such a great feature! But it only works if the data is present and accurate.
